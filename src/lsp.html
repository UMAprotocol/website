LSP Contract

The UMA Long Short Pair (LSP) contract template allows for the creation of unliquidatable & capped-payout token products. The LSP contract powers call/put options, range tokens, and KPI Options; it can be customized in other ways.

Who is it for?

DAOs that want to avail themselves of UMAâ€™s suite of treasury management products.

Financial engineers that want to create customized products that have a 0-1 range payout, such as with prediction markets or binary options.

Developers who want to customize a financial product on UMA.

How is it better?

Compared to EMP contracts on UMA, the LSP contracts are non-liquidatable and do not require running bots.

The dual token design means that sponsor positions are transferable.

Examples

A development team wants to express a crypto dominance pair, and creates a LSP that expresses the relative value of an asset compared to the entire crypto marketcap.

A booky launches a sports prediction market using the LSP contracts. The long/short tokens each represent the likelihood of the outcome of a particular game.

A stablecoin DAO uses the LSP to create a token that measures the divergence from the peg, and caps the payout at +/-20% to make it capital efficient.
