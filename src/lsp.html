LSP Contract

The LSP contract template allows for the creation of non-liquidatable and capped payout products via tokenized long and short positions.

Developers can choose between Binary, Linear, Covered Call and Range LSP contract templates.

Why create a LSP token?

Create customized products that have a 0-1 payout, suited for prediction markets or binary options.

Create long and short tokens with a variable payout based on where settlement occurs within a defined range (e.g. KPI Options).

Create Covered Call/Put Options whereby the payout is based on if a settlement price is above or below a defined strike price.

Create structured products enabling treasuries to borrow stablecoins against their projectâ€™s native token while offering unique risk profiles to investors.

How is it better?

No risk of liquidation.

Does not require running liquidator and dispute bots.

Tokenized and transferable sponsor positions.

Examples

A development team wants to express a crypto dominance pair. They create a LSP that expresses the relative value of an asset compared to the entire crypto market cap.

A booky launches a sports prediction market using the LSP contracts. The long/short tokens each represent the likelihood of the outcome of a particular game.

A stablecoin DAO uses the LSP to create a token that measures the divergence from its dollar peg and caps the payout at +/-20% to make it capital efficient.
