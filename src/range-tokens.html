Range Tokens

A range token is a treasury primitive that enables DAOs to access funds and diversify their treasury without directly selling their native tokens.
DAOs can use their native tokens as collateral to borrow funds with no risk of liquidation.

Who is it for?

DAOs with outsized treasuries in their native token, who have high exposure to market swings.

Treasury managers who want to borrow stablecoins against a project token without the immediate regulatory implications of conducting a sale.

DAOs that need to prepare their treasury by diversifying. This ensures they can fund their operations and achieve their protocol objectives.

How is it better?

DAOs can add stablecoins to their balance sheet without having to conduct a token sale, which can cause immeidate sell pressure and regulatory concerns.

This mechanism ensures that tokens are not sold too cheaply, while offering an attractive yield to farmers.

This mechanism is available to any DAO in a decentralized way, without any jurisdictional or centralized authority hoops to jump through.

Examples

UNI needs to diversify its treasury because its holdings are almost entirely denominated in its native token, so it creates a range bond to raise $10M today without any immediate $UNI sell pressure.

A DAO wants to offer a loan to developer teams building token projects on their protocol, and does so by purchasing range tokens from the teams.
