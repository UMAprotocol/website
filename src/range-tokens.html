Range Tokens

Range tokens allows DAOs to use their native tokens as collateral to borrow stablecoins (similar to convertible debt in traditional finance).

Range token pays a yield to tokenholders and only provides upside and downside exposure to the project token on significant moves. At maturity, if the debt is not paid, the range tokenholder is compensated with an equivalent amount of collateral.

Range token = Yield Dollar – Put Option + Call Option

Why create range tokens?

Diversify treasuries without selling a project’s native token(s).

Offer products with unique risk profiles best suited to investors who believe in the long term success of a project.

Offer exposure to customizable and flexible risk profiles.

How is it better?

Borrow funds without the risk of liquidation through a project’s native token

Pay a yield to investors with limited upside and downside exposure to a project’s token.

Enable flexible and customizable risk exposure strategies.

Delay the sale of native tokens to bet on a DAO’s future success.

Examples

AAVE needs to diversify its treasury because its holdings are denominated in its native token. It creates a range token to raise $10M today without immediate sell pressure of $AAVE.

A DAO offers a loan to development teams building token projects on their protocol by purchasing range tokens from the teams.
