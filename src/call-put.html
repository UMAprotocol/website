Call/Put Options

Call and Put options allow DAO treasuries to offer yield opportunities to tokenholders, as well as leverage opportunities to speculators. The options are traded against the project token, so DAOs can typically create liquidity without any opportunity cost.

Who is it for?

DAOs create these products and offer liquidity so tokenholders benefit from their features.

Community managers use them to offer incentive aligned bounties to builders, because they only pay out under certain positive circumstances.

Treasury managers sell them as a way to raise money with the treasury without selling at a bad price.

How is it better?

DAOs can launch these products and pair them against their project token. This has no volume or other requirements as on a CEX.

Liquidity for these options can come straight from the otherwise idle treasury.

Call options can be offered as incentives. They can be offered more liberally than unrestricted tokens, since they will only cost the treasury anything if the token trading high.

Examples

UMA creates a call option with a $25 strike. The community is offered these tokens as an incentive to increase awareness. The price rallies to $30, and each call option is worth $5 of UMA each.

Take the above example, except the token fails to rally. The call options expire valueless, and have no cost to the UMA treasury.
